New User interface!

Team collaboration feature!

Now, your team can post roles, like/dislike, and collaborate on the Applicant Tracking System (ATS). Not only that, but you can also give feedback on candidates! We believe this will streamline your hiring process, making it more efficient than ever before.

Applicant tracking system (ATS)

All-new ATS has been designed from the ground up to become more user-friendly, efficient, and powerful. You can now disqualify and qualify candidates. We redesigned the stages to make it just like your actual recruitment process. You can now send emails to candidates directly. And finally, we redesigned what the talent profile looks like to become a great alternative to CV's.

Actively hiring

You can now trigger a role as "actively hiring, " meaning it will automatically keep sourcing candidates until you decide to stop.

Referral tool

A new referral tool has been developed. This tool lets you invite other companies to join Tapflow, and once they hire someone for a role, you both get 50% off your roles. How exciting is that? We believe this is a great way to help you save money and make hiring even easier.
